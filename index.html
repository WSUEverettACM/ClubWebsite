About CougHacks

CougHacks is a 24-hour software centric event organized by WSU Everett in which small teams of students work towards solving interesting challenges. The goal of CougHacks is to create an inclusive environment where participants from diverse backgrounds can collaborate towards common goals. CougHacks aims to expose participants to new technologies and real world problems that they may not be exposed to in academia.

CougHacks FAQ

CougsHacks is a collegiate level hackathon held at WSU Everett.

CougsHacks will be hosted at the WSU building (915 N. Broadway, Everett, WA 98201) in North Everett

All students attending any University, College, or High School can attend.

CougHacks is completely free to attend.

All students are recomended to bring a student ID to be checked in. Additionally students should bring a computer, power strip, toothbrush, change of clothes, and we highly recommend bring a sleeping bag or blanket.

Team size is to be determined at the current momment.

In the weeks leading up to the event we will hosting activites on our social media to encourage people to form teams. Additionally, during the start of the event we will host a team forming activity to ensure that all attendies have a team.

We will preform brainstorming activities for teams that do not have predetermined project ideas.

No, we highly encourage everyone start their project during the hackathon.

Unfortunately, due to budget constraints this year CougHacks will not be able to provide travel reimbursement.

Please reach out to us via one of our social media platforms or send us an email. We are happy to answer any questions you may have.

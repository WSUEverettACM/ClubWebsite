We are The Association for Computing Machinery chapter at Washington State University Everett. Our mission is to provide our students with resources to develop their professional and technical abilities.

ABOUT

The Association for Computing Machinery is non-profit international learning society for computing. It’s the largest scientific and educational computing society in the world. The WSU Everett chapter aims to prioritizes the professional growth of our members by providing opportunities for students to supplement their professional growth through hosting events and helping members attend events. We want to help students to be prepared for life after graduation to ease the transition between school and industry. These sort of events can help round out students and build a resume to help get job opportunities.
